A — Three-Column: Players | Board | Actions

Left sidebar: player list with money. Center: full board. Right sidebar: turn actions + activity log. Classic layout, everything visible at once. Best for desktop. Mobile collapses sidebars into drawers.

B — Full-Width Board + Bottom Bar

Board takes maximum space. Thin top bar: game name, turn, money, settings. Bottom bar: action buttons (Roll, End Turn, Trade, Build). Player info appears on hover/tap of tokens on board. Best for immersion. Great on both desktop and mobile.
