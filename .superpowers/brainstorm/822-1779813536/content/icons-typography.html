B — SVG Line Icons (Lucide/Heroicons)

Clean, consistent, professional. Gold stroke icons on dark background. Same visual weight across all icons. Scales perfectly on any screen. Adds ~15KB to bundle. Looks premium, not playful.

Typography

Current fonts: Inter (body), Rajdhani (headings), JetBrains Mono (mono). Do we keep or change?

A

Keep Current Stack

Inter (body) + Rajdhani (headings) + JetBrains Mono (mono). Already loaded, works well. Rajdhani has Indian character support and a modern geometric feel.

B

Switch to Playfair + Inter

Playfair Display (serif headings) gives a luxury feel that matches gold-on-dark. Inter stays for body. More elegant, less techy. Think premium Indian restaurant menu, not gaming dashboard.
